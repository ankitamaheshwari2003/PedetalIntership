 A bicycle, also called a pedal cycle, bike, 
 push-bike or cycle, is a human-powered 
 or motor-assisted, pedal-driven, single-track 
 vehicle, with two wheels attached to a frame,
 one behind the other. A bicycle rider is called
 a cyclist, or bicyclist.
 Bicycles were introduced in the 19th century in 
 Europe. By the early 21st century there were
 more than 1 billion bicycles.There are many
 more bicycles than cars.Bicycles are the 
 principal means of transport in many regions.
